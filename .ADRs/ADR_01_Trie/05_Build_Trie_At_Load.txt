WHY THIS PHASE EXISTS
Dual-populating first lets the trie be built and validated under realistic load
conditions while the dicts still serve the runtime path, so the reader switch in
Phase 6 is a pure, reversible change. It also gives a natural place to assert
trie==dict consistency during development.
