WHY THIS PHASE EXISTS
Until now the dicts were retained as a safety net and equivalence oracle. Once
the trie path is trusted (all golden + equivalence tests green through Phase 6/7),
the dicts and ad-hoc walkers are pure overhead — memory at load and cognitive
load in the code. Removing them realizes the memory win and leaves a single
source of truth.
