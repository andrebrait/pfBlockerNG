WHY THIS PHASE EXISTS
After Phase 6 each enabled category still triggers its own descent of the trie
(data, zone, white, and on the noAAAA/AAAA path, noaaaa). For a domain with N
labels that is up to ~N node-steps per category. Splitting the query into labels
ONCE and descending ONCE, collecting every category hit along the path, removes
the redundant traversals — the main motivation for a trie over the dicts.
